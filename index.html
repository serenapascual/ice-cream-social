faq

can you tell me more?

1. chemo patients often experience mouth sores the days after receiving an infusion.

doctors recommend sucking on ice cubes to reduce bloodflow to the mouth during infusion, consequently reducing chemo exposure to the sensitive cells of the mouth lining.

(((but we think ice cream is more fun)))

2. chemo patients are often encouraged to eat when they can. ice cream provides necessary calories during treatment.

3. chemo is tough!!! we hope chemo patients can have a sweet treat to look forward to when they go to the hospital.
